About Dharit

Dharit was conceptualized after witnessing numerous relationship breakdowns that could have been prevented with access to therapy. The need for a safe space, unbiased counseling, and a secure environment became apparent. Dharit aims to provide exactly that—a sanctuary where individuals and couples, including those in LGBTQ relationships, can freely express themselves, knowing they are heard without judgment. The studio's ethos is rooted in creating a secure environment where people feel understood and supported. Every counselor at Dharit provides unbiased guidance, ensuring that clients, regardless of sexual orientation or gender identity, feel respected and empowered on their journey to healing and growth.
